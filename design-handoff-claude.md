# w² Agência — Design Handoff for Claude Design

## What This Is

This is a brand design brief for **w² Agência**, a digital presence agency for Brazilian small businesses (MEIs and microempresas). The brand direction was already approved by the client — code name **"Explosão"**. You are free to experiment with layouts, compositions, and visual details, but the core identity below is fixed.

---

## Brand DNA

**Personality:** Bold, energetic, direct. No fluff. A brand that occupies space and demands attention without apology. The visual equivalent of a confident handshake.

**Target client:** Brazilian MEIs, small shops, local businesses. People who need results fast and don't have time for big-agency bureaucracy.

**Voice:** "Você fala com quem faz." (You talk to the people who do the work.) Direct, no-nonsense, slightly irreverent.

**Mood keywords:** Ousado · Disruptivo · Energético · Brasileiro · Direto

## Non-Negotiable: Typography

- **Headings:** Barlow Condensed — weight 900 (Black), uppercase, tight leading (0.9–1.0)
- **Subheadings:** Barlow Condensed — weight 700, uppercase
- **Body text:** Barlow — weight 400/500/600
- **Italic emphasis:** Barlow Condensed Italic 900 in Magenta for maximum punch

Available via Google Fonts: `Barlow Condensed` + `Barlow`

## Logo — Core Mark (Fixed)

The logo is a typographic mark. The structure:

```
W²  |  AGÊNCIA
```

- **W** — Barlow Condensed Black, very large
- **²** — Barlow Condensed Black, superscript, in Magenta (#E01A5A)
- **|** — thin vertical rule separator
- **AGÊNCIA** — Barlow Condensed Bold, tracked wide

### Variations
1. **Dark background:** W in Yellow, ² in Magenta, AGÊNCIA in White
2. **Light background:** W in Black, ² in Magenta, AGÊNCIA in Black
3. **Yellow background:** W in Black, ² in Black (or small Magenta accent), underline in Black
4. **Icon/Avatar:** Black square, W in Yellow, ² in Magenta — for social profiles

## Design Principles (from approved direction)

1. **High contrast is the default.** Yellow on black. Black on yellow. Never gray-on-gray.
2. **Borders as design elements.** 3–4px solid borders on cards, sections, buttons. No soft shadows.
3. **Typography is structure.** Headings do layout work — no decorative illustrations needed.
4. **Energy through geometry.** Rectangular cuts, no rounded corners except on avatars. Hard edges.
5. **Accents are surgical.** Magenta is used sparingly — a word, a number, a line — never as a fill color for large areas (except CTAs on dark).

## Areas Open for Experimentation

These elements were not fully explored in the original presentation and are fair game for creative interpretation:

- **Textures and patterns** — rough paper, halftone dots, screen print grain. Could add craft/artisanal energy.
- **Diagonal cuts** — angled sections between hero and next section (instead of straight horizontal lines)
- **Background patterns** — subtle repeating W² watermark, grid lines, or graphic tape textures
- **Motion-ready design** — if designing for web, elements can suggest motion (tilted badges, arrow clusters, kinetic type)
- **"Stamp" or "seal" elements** — circular or diamond-shaped badges (e.g., "Diagnóstico Grátis" stamp)
- **Alternative icon shape** — the W² mark inside a diamond or hexagon instead of a square
- **Color blocking on mobile** — full-bleed yellow top half / black bottom half split
- **Italic headlines** — leaning more into Barlow Condensed Italic as a primary display style (not just accents)
